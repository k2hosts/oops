Privacy Policy

We believe AI infrastructure should be a pipe, not a bucket. We transfer your data, process it, and then forget it.

1. Data Retention

We practice Zero Retention for inference data.

  • Prompts & Completions: Your message history and the AI's responses are processed in-memory. They are not saved to a database, logs, or persistent storage on our servers after the request is completed.
  • Uploaded Documents: Documents sent to the API are parsed immediately and injected into the temporary context window. They are discarded instantly after the inference is finished.

2. Model Training

We do not use your data to train, fine-tune, or improve our models. Your inputs remain yours.

3. Logs & Analytics

We keep minimal technical logs to maintain service health:

  • Metadata: We store timestamps, token usage counts, and IP addresses for rate-limiting purposes.
  • Errors: If the API crashes, we may log the error code and stack trace to fix the bug, but we scrub user content from these logs where possible.

3.1 Identity & Key Management

To prevent service abuse and ensure fair usage, we utilize a tiered identity model:

  • Identity Provider: We use Discord as our primary identity gate. When you request a key, our system logs your Discord Username and User ID to issue and manage your unique API Key.
  • Separation of Concerns: Your Discord identity is stored separately from our inference engine. While our "Keylog" tracks usage metrics (tokens used, timestamps, and rate limits) linked to your Discord username, it does not record the content of your prompts or AI responses.
  • Non-Linkability: Once a request enters our "Pipe" via the Playground or API, the inference process only recognizes your API Key. Your specific conversation data is never attached to your Discord profile in our permanent records.

4. Third-Party Processors

Our infrastructure runs on Cloudflare Workers. Inference may be routed through upstream providers for execution. We ensure these providers are configured to not retain data for training purposes where applicable.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact oopsapi@tuta.io.
