What it is
01
One endpoint
POST your messages, get a response. That's the whole API. No setup, no SDK required, no reading a 40-page doc.
02
Conversation context
Pass the message history with each request. The model keeps track of the thread for as long as you need.
03
Fast
Sub-second time to first token. Built to feel instant in a UI — no spinners, no waiting around.
We believe AI should be invisible. Just a pipe between your code and the intelligence. No prompt injection guards that break your logic, no "I cannot do that", no heavy SDKs. Just raw inference.

// Unopinionated

We don't inject system prompts behind your back. You control the context, the tone, and the constraints. Your prompt is the only truth.

// Latency first

Every millisecond counts in a UI. We optimize for Time-to-First-Token (TTFT) above all else, running on bare metal GPUs close to the backbone.

// Zero retention

We do not train on your data. Request logs are ephemeral and wiped automatically. Privacy is the default, not an option.
